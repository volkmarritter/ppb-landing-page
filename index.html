Features

Domain logic that took years to build, ready in seconds

Every module reflects real investment decisions made by financial professionals — not generic AI templates.

🏠

Dynamic Home Bias Logic

Automatically adjusts regional allocation weights based on the investor's base currency. A CHF mandate gets different home bias treatment than a EUR or USD mandate — just as it should be in practice.

💱

Currency Hedging Module

Optionally includes structured guidance for assessing currency risk on non-base-currency positions, embedded directly in the generated prompt.

🗂️

6 Configurable Asset Classes

Choose from Cash, Bonds, Equities, Commodities, Listed Real Estate, and Crypto — with the latter two available as optional satellite positions for mandates that call for them.

⚠️

Synthetic ETF Evaluation

Optionally evaluates swap-based ETF alternatives where structural advantages apply — particularly relevant for optimising US dividend withholding tax exposure.

✅

Plausibility Validation

Built-in checks catch inconsistencies between risk profile and investment horizon before the prompt is generated, so your AI inputs stay coherent.

🌐

Bilingual Output

Generate prompts in German or English — ideal for DACH-based teams working with international clients or multilingual advisory workflows.

🔬

ETF Look-Through Analysis

Instructs the AI to analyse the underlying exposures across all funds — flagging hidden concentrations and overlapping holdings before they become a problem.

🏷️

TER Estimation

The generated prompt asks the AI to calculate an estimated total expense ratio for the proposed portfolio — full cost transparency.
